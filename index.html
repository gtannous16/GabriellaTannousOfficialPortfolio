    • About Me

    Gabriella Tannous was raised by her loving parents in Sugar Land, Texas. She studied Business Marketing at The University of St. Thomas in Houston, Texas. During her time in college she interned in the marketing office for Saks Fifth Avenue.

    On June 16, 2013, Gabriella married her husband and supported him as he answered his calling to the Orthodox Christian Priesthood. Since then, they have lived in New York and Fort Lauderdale, before finally settling down in Atlanta, Georgia where they currently live with their adorable dog Kallie!

    Gabriella is the Youth Director of the Diocese of Miami & the Southeast in the Antiochian Orthodox Christian Archdiocese, where she enjoys working with teenagers. She wholeheartedly believes that the youth is the future!

    Gabriella loves to be on her computer spending hours geeking out and writing code. She also loves reading, watching movies, building LEGO’s and has a serious obsession with Harry Potter! Gabriella’s passion for creativity and artistry push her to be the most unique and driven individual, eager to make her dreams a reality.
